# Direct Observatory Home — Design Revision

## Decision

The home page opens directly on the functional Publication × Journey interface. Remove the slogan hero, research summary, research cards, publication section title, explanatory copy, decorative legend, and slogan footer.

## First Viewport

- Keep one compact header for identity and contact/profile navigation.
- Place the research-area filters immediately below the header.
- Show the publication list and interactive conference globe without introductory content.
- Preserve publication filtering, globe focus, conference grouping, responsive behavior, and the compact contact links at the bottom.

## Content Rule

Every visible block must either identify Wen Sheng Lim, navigate to a real profile/contact route, expose publication data, or operate the conference journey. No positioning slogans or abstract research summaries.
